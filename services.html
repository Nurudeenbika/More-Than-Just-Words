Speech and Language Assessment
Offered for:
-
young children not yet talking or are using
minimal words
-
children who are hard to understand when
talking (speech sound errors)
-
school-aged children who have trouble
understanding and/or use language to
express themselves
-
children who have trouble with reading
and/or writing
-
Speech and Language assessment report
for autism spectrum disorder diagnosis
